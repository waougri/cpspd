About Crown Point Consulting

Founded by Malinda Elammari, a Clinical Education Specialist with extensive experience in sterile processing, Crown Point Consulting is dedicated to elevating the standards of sterile processing in healthcare facilities across the country.

With certifications from both CBSPD and IAHCSMM, and active involvement in AAMI, Malinda brings a wealth of knowledge and practical expertise to every audit and consultation. Our mission is to help healthcare facilities maintain the highest levels of patient safety through meticulous sterile processing practices.

We combine technical expertise with a commitment to education, ensuring that your team not only meets compliance standards but understands the critical importance of every step in the sterile processing workflow.
